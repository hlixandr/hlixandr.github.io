About me

I am a graduate student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation at the University of Amsterdam. I have a background in Computer Science, but during my studies I gradually shifted towards more philosophical issues. Currently, my main interests are within Philosophy of Logic, Philosophical Logic, (Formal) Epistemology and Philosophy of Science. I sometimes return to my roots and dabble into the more mathematical/computational side of logic, mainly Homotopy Type Theory.
